BEST MOVIES OF ALL TIME ACCORDING TO HASNI

MY TOP 3 MOVIES

HARRY POTTER

Stories about magic always captivating especially for this one, the character development, the progress of the story and the plot is the best.

THE SPIDERWICK CHRONICLES

It's about a family that move to Spiderwick Estate and then uncover the secrets about the estate. This movie full of fantasy with fairies and other creatures. This is a good old movie that i watch when i was a kid.

PITCH PERFECT

It's about an acapella campus group with a low reputation that stuck in their old habits. i like the character development and the persistence of the main character. If u like musical movies, u can try this one.
